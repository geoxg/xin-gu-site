Teaching Philosophy

As a scholar at the intersection of Geography, GIS, Spatial Data Science, Crime Analysis, and Human-Environment Interaction, I view teaching and research as deeply intertwined and mutually reinforcing.

My teaching philosophy is grounded in three principles: accessibility, engagement through real-world applications, and the integration of research with instruction.
